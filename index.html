About

I'm a Registered Tax agent with the Tax Practitioner's Board and have been practising for more than 30 years.

When I wasn't working with my clients, I was developing the next generation of Accounting professionals as a Lecturer in Tax and Accounting at Victoria University and Footscray Tafe.

Services

Personal tax returns are my passion and a specialty, but I can help you with BAS lodgements, Rental Property Returns, Partnership Returns and Company Returns.
